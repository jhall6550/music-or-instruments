# Instruments

This page gives short explanations of a few common instruments.

## Guitar
- String instrument played by strumming or plucking.
- Electric guitars use pickups and amplifiers.

## Piano
- Keyboard instrument; striking strings with hammers.
- Wide range and used in many styles from classical to jazz.

## Drums
- Provide the rhythm and backbeat.
- Drum kits usually include snare, bass drum, toms, hi-hat and cymbals.
