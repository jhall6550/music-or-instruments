# Jazz

Jazz began in the early 20th century and is known for improvisation, complex harmonies, and swing rhythms. Common instruments include saxophone, trumpet, piano, double bass, and drums.

## Important ideas
- Improvisation: musicians create solos on the spot.
- Harmony: jazz often uses extended chords (7ths, 9ths, etc.).
- Styles: swing, bebop, modal jazz, fusion.
