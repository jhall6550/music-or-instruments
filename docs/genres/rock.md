# Rock

Rock music arose in the 1950s and has many subgenres including classic rock, punk, and alternative. Typical elements include electric guitar, strong backbeat (drums), bass, and expressive vocals.

## Listening examples
- Short, upbeat riffs and simple chord progressions.

## Fun facts
- The electric guitar and amplifier allowed new tones and volume.
- Rock often emphasizes strong rhythms and catchy hooks.
